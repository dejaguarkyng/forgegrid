# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Provide actionable, technical feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and users.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks.
- Trolling, insults, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may remove comments, issues, PRs, or other contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.

## Reporting

Report incidents privately through the process in SECURITY.md. Include links, timestamps, and relevant context so maintainers can investigate.
